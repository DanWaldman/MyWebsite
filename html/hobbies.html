Hobbies

The Beach

Growing up in Netanya, near the beach, made my childhood amazing. The Beach is one of my favourite places. I like going fishing, swimming, running by the beach - and generally I believe all activities when done at the beach - are just BETTER.

Sports

As a young boy I used to play basketball, baseball, football and even golf. With the passing of the years I stayed at the basketball court and played in "Irony Netanya" - my city's basketball team. Today I mostly work out at the gym and sometimes play basketball with friends.

Programming

Programming has always been some kind of hobby of mine - which I hope to turn into my profession. I learned C# and python in high school, then did some interesting online courses and projects in python. Now I'm in my computer science degree - and I enjoy the programming courses very much.

Travel

I love traveling. After my army service I went to a summer camp in Pennsylvania, from there I went to Virginia, Los Vegas and Colorado. From there I flew to Peru, and then Bolivia, then Argentina, then Uruguay, Brazil - and I finished skying in Canada. All together my trip was 9 months.
