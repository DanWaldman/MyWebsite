Employment

2013 - 2015 | Madrich And Manager In The "Maccabi Hatzair" Youth Movement

In those years I was a madrich in my neighborhood youth movement, I taught kids and teens about all kinds of values, important principles and camping skills.

2016 | Year Of Service In Ramat Gan

During this year I lived in a commune and volunteered with teens from tough backgrounds - helping them with school, afternoon activities, and issues they had. I also managed a branch of "Maccabi Hatzair" in Or Yehuda.

2019 | Madrich In A Summer Camp In Pennsylvania

In the summer of 2019 - right after I finished my army service, I was a Shaliach from the jewish agency, as a Madrich in the BBYO Perlman camp in Pennsylvania. The BBYO is a sister movement with "Maccabi Hatzair", and I was sent thanks to my history with the movement. After the summer camp, I went on a journey starting from the US, all through the countries of South America, and then finished up in Canada - it was a BLAST.
