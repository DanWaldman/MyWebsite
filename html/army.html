Army Service

2016 | Electronic Warfare Operator

I served in the IDF Electronic Warfare Unit (Communication Corps). It started with 8 months of physical training and studying communications of all kinds, the electromagnetic spectrum, and ways of attacking and manipulating radio frequencies.

2018 | Training in Elbit Systems

Training in the offices of Elbit in Holon as a representative of the IDF EW unit. The course included training on EW systems that Elbit developed specifically for the unit and performing experiments and tests on the system under laboratory conditions.

2018 | Commander and System coordinator

At the end of the course and the testing process, I was appointed a special Commander in the unit, trained the unit's soldiers on the systems and trained representatives of foreign military forces as part of IDF’s collaboration with various foreign military forces.
